How It Works

A senior ads team — without the cost of hiring one.

We run your Google and Facebook ads end-to-end: strategy, setup, creative, and weekly tuning. You focus on your product. We focus on getting you more sales.

01

Free audit (Week 1–2)

We open up your Google Merchant Center, your campaigns, your product feed, your tracking, and your creative. We tell you exactly what's broken — before spending a single euro more.

02

Rebuild your campaigns (Week 2–4)

We restructure everything around what makes you actual profit — not just revenue. Google Shopping. Search. Performance Max. Facebook and Instagram prospecting and retargeting. Every campaign tied to a clear target.

03

Weekly tuning (ongoing)

Bid adjustments. Bad keywords removed. Audiences refreshed. Creatives rotated. Real weekly attention — not a monthly glance at a dashboard.

Fresh creatives every month

Our designer ships new image and video ads every month. We test what works, kill what doesn't. No more six-month-old ads dragging your costs up.

05

Honest monthly reporting

Real numbers each month: revenue, cost per sale, what's working, what we're testing next. Plain English. No vanity metrics. No spin.
