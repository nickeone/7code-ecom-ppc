How It Works

A senior PPC team, at a fraction of the cost of hiring in-house.

We take full ownership of your paid channels — strategy, execution, creative direction, and reporting. You stay focused on your product.

01

Account Audit & Onboarding (Week 1–2)

We review your Google Merchant Center, campaign structure, feed quality, tracking setup, and creative performance. We identify the top 5 revenue leaks before spending a single extra euro.

We rebuild campaigns around your margins — not just revenue. Shopping, Search, Performance Max on Google. Prospecting and retargeting on Meta. Every campaign with a clear ROAS target.

Our designer produces fresh static and video ad creatives monthly. We test systematically, cut what's not working, and scale what is. No ad fatigue.

Clear numbers: ROAS, CPA, revenue attributed, spend breakdown, next 30-day plan. No jargon. No spin. Just results and what comes next.
